### ⚙️ How to Enable the Developer Tab in Excel

Before you can use VBA macros, you may need to enable the hidden Developer tab in Excel. 

**For Windows:**
1. Open Excel and click on `File` > `Options`.
2. Click on `Customize Ribbon` in the left sidebar.
3. In the list on the right side (under "Main Tabs"), check the box next to **Developer**.
4. Click `OK`.

**For Mac:**
1. Open Excel and click on `Excel` > `Preferences` in the top menu bar.
2. Click on `Ribbon & Toolbar`.
3. In the "Main Tabs" list, check the box next to **Developer**.
4. Click `Save` or simply close the window.
